## Decisions Made
- EventRecordingViewModel promoted to singleton: Required for HsAndTsPage and MainPage to share the same HsAndTsItems collection. Without singleton, each tab would get a separate instance with separate state.
- HistorialPage binds directly to IEventLogService: Per research pitfall #4, avoids tight coupling between HistorialPage and EventRecordingViewModel. IEventLogService is already a singleton and exposes the Events collection directly.
- RhythmSelector button text "ASISTOLIA" uppercased: Consistent with all-uppercase button style (RCE, AESP, TV, FV are all uppercase).
