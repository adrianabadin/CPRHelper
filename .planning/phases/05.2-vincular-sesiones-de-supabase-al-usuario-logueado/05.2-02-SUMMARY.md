## Decisions Made

- **Logout cleanup in AuthViewModel, not OnAuthStateChanged:** After `SignOutAsync()` completes, `IAuthService.CurrentUserId` is already null, so the handler has no userId to act on. The ViewModel retains the userId before calling sign-out and can invoke `DeleteLocalUserSessionsAsync` explicitly.
- **IDispatcherTimer for retry timer:** MAUI requires UI-thread-safe timers in singletons. `Application.Current?.Dispatcher.CreateTimer()` is the correct cross-platform API.
- **Max 5 retry attempts per item:** Prevents infinite queue growth from persistently failing uploads (e.g., RLS policy violation). Items are silently dropped after 5 attempts with a Debug.WriteLine log.
- **Backoff resets to 30s on full queue success:** Ensures aggressive retries restart quickly after a connectivity gap resolves.
